Policies



Financing

1.developing an annual budget.
2.monitoring and making budget adjustments during the year.
3.monitoring the performance of investments.
4.selection and collaboration with a CPA/auditor
5.The board will adopt a recommended record retention and document destruction schedule, including checks, receipts, tax returns, etc.


Delivery

1.A service fee is applied to orders delivered by UPS, however the actual service fees charged may vary. For example, same day deliveries, weekend and holiday service fees may be higher.
- For residential deliveries, if the recipient is not available at the time of delivery, the delivery person may leave the floral arrangement for the recipient to retrieve when they return or attempt delivery the next day. During major holidays, deliveries may be made as late as 9 pm.
- For business deliveries, if the business is closed or not accepting deliveries, delivery will be attempted the next business day. During holidays, deliveries may be made as late as 5pm.
2.Early delivery may be available for select zip codes. Early delivery shipments will be delivered by 12 noon on the requested delivery date (due to high demand, holiday deliveries are guaranteed by 1:30 pm). Shipping Fees for these items vary based on the product amount.
3.erishable products will require Next-Day delivery.
4.Please include apartment, unit, or room number in the delivery address, if applicable. Deliveries to colleges and universities may take additional time.
5.Surcharges apply to delivery to Alaska and Hawaii. Taxes are based on prevailing rates in the delivery area.

Return Policies

Most unopened items in new condition and returned within 90 days will receive a refund or exchange. Some items have a modified return policy noted on the receipt or in the item department. Items that are opened or damaged or do not have a receipt may be denied a refund or exchange.
